Support

Quick Help

Most issues can be solved with these quick fixes:

  • 1.Not transcribing? Make sure you've granted microphone permissions in Settings → Voicepaste → Microphone
  • 2.Low accuracy? Speak clearly and reduce background noise. Try holding the phone closer.
  • 3.App crashing? Force close and reopen. If that doesn't work, reinstall the app.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enable the dyslexia-friendly font?

Go to Settings (gear icon) → Accessibility → Toggle "OpenDyslexic Font" on.

Why is there a daily limit on the free version?

AI transcription costs money to run. The free tier lets you try Voicepaste before committing. PRO users get unlimited transcriptions.

Is my voice data stored?

No. Your audio is transcribed and immediately deleted. We never store your voice recordings. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Does it work offline?

Currently, Voicepaste requires an internet connection for transcription. Offline mode is planned for a future update.

How do I restore my PRO purchase?

Go to Settings → Restore Purchase. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used to purchase.

Why is the accuracy different from advertised?

94.7% accuracy was measured in controlled testing with clear speech. Results vary based on background noise, accent, and speaking clarity.
