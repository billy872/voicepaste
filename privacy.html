Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 21, 2026

The Short Version

Your voice recordings are transcribed and immediately deleted. We don't store your audio. We don't sell your data. Period.

What We Collect

  • Audio recordings: Temporarily processed for transcription only
  • Transcribed text: Stored locally on YOUR device only
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous crash reports and basic usage stats (can be disabled)

What We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ Your voice recordings (deleted after transcription)
  • ❌ Personal information (no account required)
  • ❌ Location data
  • ❌ Contacts
  • ❌ Photos or files

Third-Party Services

Voicepaste uses third-party AI services for transcription:

  • Groq API: Processes audio for transcription. Audio is not stored by Groq after processing.

Data Storage

All transcription history is stored locally on your device. We never upload or sync your transcriptions to any server. If you delete the app, your data is gone.

Children's Privacy

Voicepaste does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Since we don't store your data, there's nothing to delete. Your transcription history is on your device — delete it anytime from the app or by removing the app.

Contact Us

Questions about privacy? Email us at billy@siddatwork.com

Changes to This Policy

We'll update this page if our privacy practices change. Major changes will be announced in the app.
